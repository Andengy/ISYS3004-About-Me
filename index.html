About me

When I'm not jetting off to different destinations or creating drinks for people, you'll often find me indulging in my love for sports, particularly volleyball and badminton, where I thrive on the camaraderie and adrenaline of the game. But that's not all, I'm also a gearhead at heart.

There's something incredibly satisfying about rolling up my sleeves and tinkering away on my car, fine-tuning every detail until it purrs like a kitten. Whether it's a weekend project or an ongoing passion, I find joy in the challenge of mastering the mechanics and unleashing the full potential of my vehicle.

I am a passionate traveler, explorer, and photography enthusiast on a mission to discover the wonders of our world. Ever since I took my first trip, I've been hooked on the thrill of exploring new destinations, immersing myself in diverse cultures, and capturing unforgettable moments through my lens.

Whether I'm wandering through bustling cities, trekking across breathtaking landscapes, or unwinding on serene beaches, I'm always fueled by a sense of adventure and curiosity. Traveling has opened my eyes to the beauty and diversity of different culture, and each journey leaves me with lasting memories and unforgettable experiences.
